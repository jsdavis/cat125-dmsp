Loot Box Overview

Dice Boxes

You can purchase a dice box any time you are about to make an attack roll, with any weapon. For $1.00, you can replace your dice pool with the results of opening one of these boxes, with a chance to roll 4 or more dice! Only one dice box can be purchased per attack action.
Buy a dice box

Weapon Boxes

You can purchase weapon boxes between rounds of combat. For $2.00, open a box with potentially very powerful weapons! There is no limit to how many weapon boxes you can purchase in a single game, so keep trying to get the best gear!
Buy a weapon box

Deployment Boxes

Deployment boxes can be purchased either at the beginning of a game or when redeploying a group. For just $3.00, you have a chance to replace a lowly stormtrooper group with the legendary Darth Vader! Just like weapon boxes, there are no limits here!
Buy a deployment box
